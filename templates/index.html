Case File №3

Methodology

How a paragraph of text becomes a verdict

I

Intake

The raw email — subject, body, embedded links and markup — is received exactly as written.

II

Feature extraction

27 measurements are taken: link patterns, keyword frequency, urgency language, capitalization, greeting style, and more.

III

Ensemble vote

200 decision trees independently weigh the evidence. The majority verdict becomes the model's output.

IV

Report

A verdict, a confidence score, and the specific evidence behind it — in plain language, not a black box.
